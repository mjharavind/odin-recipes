Karuvaadu Kulambu

Description

Karuvaadu Kulambu (Dry Fish Curry) is a popular South Indian dish, especially in Tamil Nadu. It is made with sun-dried fish (karuvaadu) cooked in a tangy tamarind-based curry with spices. The dry fish adds a unique flavor and aroma to the dish.

This curry is usually served with steamed rice and is loved for its bold and traditional taste. It is a comforting dish that remind many of home-cooked meals and family gatherings.
